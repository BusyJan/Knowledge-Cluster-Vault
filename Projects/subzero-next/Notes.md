Detected via paren-balanced walker: SW20 (ANO_Encoder, 11 pins), SW21 (BTN_BOOT, 2), SW22 (BTN_RESET, 2), U55 (MAX17048, 8), U56 (LSM6DSO, 14), ANT1 (GPS_Patch_Antenna, 2).
